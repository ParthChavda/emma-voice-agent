# Doctor & Nurse Schedules at Elmwood Road Surgery

## Dr. Ahmed

Dr. Ahmed is available for routine appointments and telephone consultations on weekday mornings from 9:00am.

## Dr. Chen

Dr. Chen holds routine appointments on weekday mornings, plus a limited number of urgent same-day slots released each morning at 8:00am.

Nurse Patel runs nurse appointments (blood tests, vaccinations, wound care, chronic disease reviews) on weekday mornings.

Appointment slots run in 30-minute blocks starting at 9:00am on weekdays. Availability changes daily as slots are booked, so exact openings are confirmed at the time of booking.
